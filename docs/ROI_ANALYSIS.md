This document provides financial projections and ROI analysis for three stakeholder groups: **Platform Operators**, **Property Managers**, and **Investors**.

## 3. Investor Economics

### Investment Thesis

Fractional real estate offers investors:
- **Lower barriers**: $100 minimum vs. $50,000+ traditional
- **Diversification**: Own shares in multiple properties
- **Liquidity**: Trade tokens vs. 6-12 month property sales
- **Transparency**: On-chain dividend history and governance

### Return Components

| Return Source | Typical Range | Notes |
|---------------|---------------|-------|
| Rental Yield | 5-8% annually | Distributed as dividends |
| Property Appreciation | 3-5% annually | Reflected in token value |
| Tax Benefits | 1-3% effective | Pass-through depreciation* |
| **Total Expected Return** | **9-16% annually** | Before fees |

*Tax benefits depend on investor situation and jurisdiction.

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|
| Smart Contract Vulnerability | High | Low | Security audits, insurance |
| Regulatory Changes | High | Medium | Legal counsel, compliance updates |
| Market Downturn | Medium | Medium | Diversification, long-term hold |
| Property Vacancy | Medium | Medium | Quality screening, reserves |
